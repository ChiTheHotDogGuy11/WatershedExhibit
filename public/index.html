Hello,

In this game you will learn about sustainable environmental features by adding them to a virtual building.
You are given a budget to purchase sustainable features for your building.

Next

Place models of the features onto the table to install them to your building.
You can hit the orange 'i' icon next to the pieces to explore their features, benefits, and installation costs.
If you don't like a feature, remove it from the screen to uninstall it.

Next

When you are happy with your building, hit 'Submit' to finalize your design.

How to Play

Welcome to Eco Touch, a environmental simulation game!

Explore each sustainable system and determine how to build the greenest and the most cost-efficient house! The next couple screens will walk you through the concept of the game.

YOUR GOAL is to determine which systems work best in your house given a specified house size and environment.

Next

Once the game begins, place and drag these pieces around the display to learn more about each system!

You have 3 rounds to explore and find the optimal system(s) for your house.

Once decided, move the system into the circular slot below the house and hit "play" to run the yearly time lapse.
